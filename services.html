Our Services

Where we work with businesses

Every business is different. The services listed here reflect the most common areas where we work, but most engagements involve more than one. If you are not sure where your situation fits, start with a conversation.

01
Strategic Management

Many successful businesses reach a point where management knows the business can grow, but is not sure when and where to start. These services help businesses make that decision and put the right foundations in place to act on it.

The business is doing well but top management is handling too much and does not always have the full picture to decide what to do next. We review how the business is organised and run today, the market it operates in, and the opportunities and challenges it is facing. Based on this, we put together recommendations for what needs to be changed and the right move going forward.

Also coversOrganisational leadership, profit centre management, performance monitoring, productivity improvement, cost control measures.

The business is running but everyone is doing things their own way. There is no standard system that everyone involved in a process can follow and see what is happening and where things stand. There is no reliable report for leadership to take the right decision at the right time.

We map how the business is currently operating, look at what information exists and what is missing, and set up the right processes, systems, and reporting tools. This covers the end-to-end functions of the business. Each function and role gets a clear set of processes so every person knows their responsibilities and how to fulfil them. We also look at what the business is spending across vendors and operations, where the margins are, and where costs can be brought down.

Also coversSOP design and implementation, MIS and management dashboards, ERP analysis and implementation support, ISO systems and operational controls.

The business is ready to grow but it is not clear if the team and the organisation are set up to support that. Many businesses reach this point without formal HR policies or a clear organisational structure in place. Staff are not always clear on what their role is or what is expected of them.

We look at the current setup, understand what is working and what is not, and put the right policies and structures in place. We also work with the team to define roles and responsibilities clearly so every person knows what they are expected to do and can do their job with confidence.

Also coversHR policy formulation, recruitment and onboarding systems, performance evaluation systems, training and development frameworks, organisational structuring, leadership development, defining job roles and responsibilities.

The business is looking to expand but it is not clear whether the current vendors and operations can support that, or what the right setup looks like in a new location. Before deciding where to expand, you need to know if your vendors can support you there and what it will cost to operate from that location.

We look at the current vendor and procurement setup, how goods are moved and at what cost, and whether the logistics network can support growth. This includes evaluating transportation options, storage and handling requirements, and whether existing vendors can service a new location or whether new ones need to be developed.

Also coversProcurement systems and vendor management, inventory management and control, stock optimisation, supply chain efficiency improvement, purchasing cost reduction.
02
Digital Transformation

Most businesses run on manual processes and verbal approvals. Digital tools fix this. The right documents reach the right people at the right time and approvals can be given from anywhere. Decisions and actions cannot be taken without the right authorisation in place. Every decision is recorded and traceable. Issues are flagged before they become a problem.

Most businesses run on manual processes and verbal approvals. A purchase order gets raised without the right approval. A decision gets delayed because the right person is not available. There is no record of what was agreed or who approved what.

Digital processes fix this. The right documents reach the right people at the right time and approvals can be given from anywhere. Decisions and actions cannot be taken without the right authorisation in place. Every decision is recorded and traceable. Issues are flagged before they become a problem.

We map how the business is currently operating, identify where manual processes are causing delays or creating gaps in accountability, and implement the right digital tools and workflows. We also look at where AI can add practical value, for example in forecasting how much stock or raw material the business will need, or in flagging unusual patterns in expenses before they become a larger issue.

Also coversProcess automation, digital workflow implementation, technology assessment and roadmap, MIS reports.
03
Operations & Change Management

Some businesses win the order but cannot deliver on time. Others start a project but cannot get it off the ground. These services are for businesses with an operations problem, whether it has already become a crisis or they can see it coming.

Many businesses find that their projects rarely finish on time or within budget. What starts as a small delay becomes a larger problem. Disputes that could have been avoided become expensive and drawn out.

We review how projects are currently being planned and managed, identify where things are going wrong, and help put the right controls and processes in place. For businesses already in a dispute or arbitration, we go through the full contract, communications, and project documentation to make sure the client has the strongest possible case and their legal team has everything they need.

For businesses that want to get ahead of these problems, we can stay involved from the start of each project so issues are caught early before they become harder to fix.

Also coversProject planning and scheduling, project cost control and budgeting, contract management, arbitration and dispute resolution support.

Some businesses know they are ready to grow but are not sure what the right move is. Others have already decided where they want to go but need help making it happen properly. We work with the business to evaluate whether expansion makes sense, what the right direction is, and what it will take to execute it.

If the decision is made to go ahead, we help set up the new unit, office, or business line with the right structure, systems, and processes in place from the start.

Also coversCompany formation and organisational structuring, setting up operations in a new location, governance frameworks, accounting systems setup, internal controls, operational manuals.

A portion of units are coming out with quality issues. Costs are going up but output is staying the same. Deliveries are getting delayed but the capacity seems to be there. These are the kinds of problems where the symptom is visible but the cause is not. It could be the production process, the way the business is organised, how goods are handled and transported, or something else.

We review how the business is currently operating, find where the problem is coming from, and implement the changes needed to fix it.

Also coversBusiness process improvement, operational restructuring, productivity improvement, cost optimisation.
04
Finance & Governance

Many businesses track the money coming in but not what it actually costs to run the business. Turnover is growing but so is the debt. Without the right checks in place, problems can go undetected for a long time.

Turnover is growing but there is never enough money when it is needed. Most businesses track what is coming in but not what it actually costs to run the business. Costs are not tracked product by product or location by location. There is no clear picture of where the money is going or whether each part of the business is making money.

We review how the business is currently managing its funds and costs, identify where funds are not being managed as well as they could be, and put the right financial management systems in place. This includes tracking costs properly, managing cash flow, and giving leadership a clear picture of where the business stands financially at any point.

Also coversFinancial planning and budgeting, funds flow and working capital management, tracking actuals against budgets, product and project costing, break-even analysis, profitability monitoring, bank and financial institution management.

GST returns filed late or TDS not deducted for certain contractors. Products sold below cost without realising the effect. Funds that were not accounted for. Approvals granted and actions taken without following company procedure. These are common problems that occur when the right checks are not in place.

We review the business across its financial, operational, and compliance processes and identify where the gaps are. We also look at whether each product or branch is actually making money, whether it is breaking even, and whether costs are higher than they need to be. Based on this, we put the right audit and control systems in place.

Also coversInternal audit systems, cost audit, operational and process audit, financial audit review, statutory and regulatory compliance monitoring.

Bills are approved by the wrong people. There are no clear limits on what can be approved and by whom. Entries are made into the books without the right checks. Each office or branch is maintaining accounts differently. When something goes wrong it is hard to trace where it started.

We review the current accounting setup, identify where the process is breaking down, and put a clear accounting SOP in place. This covers who is responsible for each type of transaction, what needs to be checked before an approval is given, how bills and expenses are verified, and how everything is documented and entered into the books correctly.

Also coversAccounting process design, internal controls, approval frameworks, accounting manuals.

That is completely normal. Start by telling us about your business. We will take it from there.
