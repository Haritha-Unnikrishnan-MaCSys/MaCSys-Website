About MaCSys

A management consultancy built around the full picture.

MaCSys Solutions Limited is a management consultancy that looks at the entire business, not just where the problem is. We bring the right expertise together and work with your team to implement the changes, not just hand over a report.

Our Story

MaCSys Solutions Limited was founded by professionals who have spent decades working with businesses across industries. Across every industry they worked in, they encountered the same problems.

Sales are growing but profit is shrinking. Every month the books are closed but nobody can authenticate the figures. One key person exits and suddenly the business unit becomes sick. Funds are being spent without anyone checking where they are going. These are just some of the situations businesses find themselves in.

In most of these situations, it is not always clear what is actually causing the issues. Because of this, businesses often end up solving the wrong problem, and the actual issue continues to exist. The same issues appear repeatedly. No SOPs, no clear policies, lack of transparency, every team doing things differently.

This works as long as the business stays small and is managed directly by the founders. Growing without structure creates its own problems. So does expanding into a new location without the right foundations in place.

Businesses also find themselves in difficult situations with contractors and clients. Disputes that could have been contained become expensive and prolonged. In many cases, businesses end up receiving less than they should have, or nothing at all, simply because the dispute was not handled well from the start. MaCSys has worked with businesses in these situations and understands what it takes to help them through.

Over the years, the team kept seeing the same problems and helping businesses work through them. At some point, it became clear that many businesses were dealing with these issues without anyone to help them see the full picture. MaCSys was built to do exactly that.

How We Work

What you can expect

Whether your business is growing steadily or going through a difficult period, we work with you to understand where things stand and what needs to change.
